# layoff-trend-analysis
Layoffs Data Cleaning &amp; Exploratory Analysis using MySQL

Cleaned and standardized 2,000+ layoff records in MySQL, ensuring consistent and reliable data for analysis of workforce reduction patterns across industries and regions.

Identified that layoffs were heavily concentrated in tech and startup sectors, indicating higher vulnerability of growth-stage companies during economic downturn periods.

Revealed clear temporal spikes in layoffs across specific years, suggesting cyclical workforce reductions that could help stakeholders anticipate future market slowdowns.

Found that US-based companies accounted for a significant share of total layoffs, highlighting regional concentration of restructuring activity in the dataset.

Developed SQL-based ranking and rolling total metrics to track top-affected companies over time, enabling comparative analysis of workforce impact across organizations
